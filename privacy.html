Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This policy explains how the Move Games games — including Monkey Dodge and Balloon Dodge (the "Apps") — handle your information. We built these Apps to respect your privacy: the gameplay happens on your device, and we do not run accounts or collect personal information directly.

The camera

The Apps use your device's front-facing camera as the controller — it reads your body's position and movement in real time so you can duck, jump, and dodge. The camera feed is processed live, entirely on your device, solely to detect your position during play. We do not record, store, or transmit the camera feed, and it never leaves your device.

Optional screen recording

You can optionally enable screen recording to capture a clip of a run and share it. This feature is off by default and only runs when you turn it on. Any clip you record is created and kept only on your device. We never upload, receive, or store your recordings. A clip is discarded unless you choose to save it to your Photos or share it through your device's share sheet — in which case it is handled by you and the destination you pick, not by us.

Advertising

The Apps display ads through Google AdMob, using non-personalized (contextual) ads only. We do not use your advertising identifier to track you across other companies' apps or websites, and there is no tracking prompt. To deliver and measure ads, Google may process limited information such as approximate (IP-based) location and your interactions with ads, and may store information on your device — but this is not used to build an advertising profile of you.

  • Where required (for example, in the European Economic Area and UK), we present a consent prompt provided by Google's User Messaging Platform before information is stored on or read from your device for ads.
  • You can limit ad tracking and reset your advertising identifier at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.

For details on how Google uses data, see Google's Privacy & Terms.

Purchases

The Apps offer a one-time "Remove Ads" in-app purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Data stored on your device

Your high scores, settings, and gameplay preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or receive this information. Removing the App removes this data.

Children

The Apps are intended for a general audience and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising is configured for a general audience content rating.

Information we do not collect

We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or camera footage. Aside from the advertising data described above (handled by Google), we do not collect personal information about you.

Your choices

  • Manage advertising consent through the prompt shown where required, and limit ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.
  • Reset your advertising identifier in iOS Settings.
  • Decline or revoke camera access in iOS Settings (note: the game needs the camera to be played).
  • Delete the App to remove all locally stored data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Apps evolve. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date.
