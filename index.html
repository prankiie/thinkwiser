Frequently asked questions

Why does the game need camera access?

The front camera is how you play — it reads your body's movement in real time so you can duck, jump, and dodge. The camera feed is processed live on your device to detect your position. It is never recorded, saved, or sent anywhere by us.

Does the game record me?

Only if you choose to. Screen recording is off by default. If you turn it on, the clip is created and stored only on your device so you can share a highlight — it is never uploaded to us, and it's discarded unless you save it to your Photos or share it yourself.

Can I play in a small space or sitting down?

Yes. You can play with your upper body in a tight space or go full-body when you have room. Just make sure the camera can see you and you have a little space to move.

How do I remove ads?

There's a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase in the app. If you've bought it before — even on another device with the same Apple ID — open the paywall and tap Restore Purchases.

What devices are supported?

iPhone with a front-facing camera, running a recent version of iOS. A few feet of space to move helps.
